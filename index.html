Recognizer Diagram Sketch (RecorDS)

Teams that use agile modeling practices often use whiteboards as they provide a dynamic and collaborative modeling environment. To assist in the generation of software artifacts, an application was developed to recognize sketches of class diagrams. For the purposes of developing this application, image processing and analysis techniques are used.
